About

A passionate musician, educator, producer, and student, Luciano is currently pursuing a Bachelors in Music Technology from Temple University. He's studied music for over a decade, and enjoys playing a wide range of styles including jazz, funk, and rock. He currently plays in several jazz and contemporary groups. Luciano has had the privilege of studying jazz guitar with leading musicians and educators, and is currently studying under Philly guitarist Jake Kelberman.

Luciano graduated with high honors from Cab Calloway School of the Arts in Wilmington, Delaware. While enrolled, he took an active role as a multi-instrumentalist in the music program. Luciano participated in the Wind Ensemble, the Jazz ensemble, the indoor drumline, and served as the Drum Major for the 'Spirit' Marching Band.
